About Us

Meet Piet Seloga — Expert Thatcher & Lodge Specialist

Expert Craftsmanship You Can Trust

Kgorogang Thatching Services is built on years of hands-on experience, traditional skills, and a strong reputation for quality work across South Africa.

Led by Piet Seloga, we specialize in high-quality thatching for lodges, game farms, lapas, and private homes. Originally from Limpopo, we are fully mobile and have successfully completed projects in multiple provinces.

Our approach combines authentic African thatching traditions with modern techniques for durability, fire safety, and long-lasting beauty. Whether it's a new roof, repairs, or a custom lapa, we deliver work that our clients are proud of.
